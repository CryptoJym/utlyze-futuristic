Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the setup cost?

Installation, training, and configuration of your AI agents and workflows; deep research on your firm and priority automations; design & development of high-ROI automations; and model training & refinement.
Note: Model training/refinement is included with Workflow Operator and Autonomy Suite. The Team Assistant tier’s $5,000 setup covers core setup & configuration (no advanced refinement).

Can I upgrade between tiers?

Absolutely! You can upgrade from Team Assistant to Workflow Operator or Autonomy Suite at any time. We'll help migrate your workflows and provide additional training as needed.

What if I need more users or integrations?

Each tier has recommended limits, but we can customize packages. Additional users are $5/user/mo. Extra integrations can be added as needed (see add-ons above).

Do you offer trials or a proof of concept?

We don’t offer trials. Instead, we co-build through iterative prototypes from day one—initial prototype → feedback → next prototype—so you can see and steer the solution at every step.

How do AI agents compare to traditional automation?

Unlike RPA or traditional automation, AI agents understand context, make decisions, and handle exceptions. They're perfect for complex, judgment-based tasks. See our use cases for examples.

Is my data secure with on-prem deployment?

Yes. Your models and data are isolated on dedicated, private infrastructure. Your data is not used to train shared models. We can meet strict isolation requirements and support compliance needs such as SOC 2 and HIPAA.
