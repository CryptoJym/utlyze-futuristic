Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the setup cost?

Setup costs cover hardware procurement, on-premise installation, initial configuration, and hands-on training for your team. We handle everything from unboxing to your first automated workflow.

Can I upgrade between tiers?

Absolutely! You can upgrade from Team Assistant to Workflow Operator or Autonomy Suite at any time. We'll help migrate your workflows and provide additional training as needed.

What if I need more users or integrations?

Each tier has recommended limits, but we can customize packages. Additional users are $50/month each. Extra integrations and workflows can be added as needed (see add-ons above).

Do you offer trials or proof of concepts?

Yes! We offer 30-day proof of concept deployments for qualified enterprises. This lets you test AI agents with your actual workflows before committing. Contact us to learn more.

How do AI agents compare to traditional automation?

Unlike RPA or traditional automation, AI agents understand context, make decisions, and handle exceptions. They're perfect for complex, judgment-based tasks. See our use cases for examples.

Is my data secure with on-premise deployment?

Yes! All processing happens on your hardware, behind your firewall. No data leaves your premises. The Autonomy Suite includes enterprise-grade security features like SSO, SCIM, and full audit trails.
